  &= 2r + r\left(2\pi - 2\arctan\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)\right) \\
  &= 2r\left(1 + \pi - \arctan\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)\right) \\ 
\frac{r}{L} &= \frac{1}{2\left(1 + \pi - \arctan\left(\frac{1}{2}\right)\right)} \\

\end{align*}
