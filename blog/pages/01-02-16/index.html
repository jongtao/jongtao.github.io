YOGO Grenade Concept Art

A few years ago in 2013, I made a game called "YOGO Grenade," featuring a girl with a machine gun mowing down evil, man-eating bunnies and profusely referencing Mony Python's Holy Grail. You can read about it in my post here.

The following is the original "Ms. Orange." The character is called "Orange," because I used an orange square as a place holder when I was writting the code before drawing the sprites. The sprite was drawn on a 32x32 pixel canvas in 10 minutes (game jam time crunch).

Figure 1. The Original Ms. Orange.

If I somehow find the time and focus, I may rewrite the game. In the meanwhile, I decided to try some concept art.
